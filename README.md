## 👋 About Me

I'm **Kundan Kumar**, a B.S. in Data Science student at **IIT Madras** and B.Tech in AI & Data Science at **Lakshmi Narain College of Technology**, building systems at the intersection of **agentic AI, retrieval-augmented generation, and explainability**.

Most of what I build tries to answer one question: **how do you make an LLM-based system reliable enough that someone else would bet on its output?** That question runs through everything below — from grounding RAG answers strictly in retrieved context, to making model decisions auditable with XAI, to giving agents memory that survives a session restart.

```text
🔭  Building     → multi-agent LLM pipelines with LangGraph (planning, tool-use, memory)
🧠  Obsessing    → getting RAG systems to say "I don't know" instead of hallucinating
🌱  Learning     → adversarial robustness & explainability (SHAP) for models people trust
🏆  Recently     → Top 15 All-India Finalist, YI BBIC 2.0 (Deep Tech) @ IIT Madras
```
